The pros of artificial intelligence

There are many advantages and benefits of using artificial intelligence, and these include the following

Permanent work

This is through the ability of machines to work continuously without feeling tired or bored

Applications that are important for daily life

Artificial intelligence provides many applications that have become important for the daily life of man, and the smart phone and its various smart systems such as GPS

Providing medical care

There are many systems of artificial intelligence that provide medical care to humans, through surgical simulators, or those applications that help to detect neurological disorders or that allow the patient to know the side effects of medications
