Types of artificial intelligence

Artificial limited intelligence One of the types of artificial intelligence that can perform specific and clear tasks, such as self-driving cars, is limited or narrow artificial intelligence..
General artificial intelligence It is the type that can work with a capacity similar to the human ability in terms of thinking, as it focuses on making the machine able to think and plan on its own and in a similar way to human thinking
Superior Artificial Intelligence For a type that may exceed the level of human intelligence, and who can do the tasks better than the specialized and knowledgeable person
